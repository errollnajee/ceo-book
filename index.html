About the Author

Dr. Erroll Najee is a leadership trainer, motivational speaker, and founder of Best2Train Counseling Services. With over two decades of experience and as former CEO of Business Emergency Safety Training (BEST), he has led statewide programs in leadership, emergency preparedness, and organizational change.

Dr. Najee holds a Ph.D. in Metaphysical Science, a BA in Human Resources Administration, and a Certificate in Small Business Development—and is currently advancing his leadership training through Dale Carnegie Associates.
